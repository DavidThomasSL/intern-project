Play with your friends on phone or computer! Crazy games for all the family to enjoy!

Get Started

No Setup Needed

Simply go to the Clonage site on your device of choice and join a room, no App or Installation required.

Invite Your Friends

Choose to create a new room, and share the room code with your friends to let them join! Join someone else's room using their room code.

No Connection, No Problem!

If you lose connection or accidently refresh the page, no need to worry. AS soon as you connect again you'll be put right back into the action where you left off!
